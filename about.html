Who we are

At EasyCooking, we are passionate advocates for the art of cooking and the joy it brings to people's lives. As the proud owners of a vibrant cooking blog website, we have cultivated a community where culinary enthusiasts of all skill levels come together to explore the diverse world of gastronomy. Our team is comprised of seasoned chefs, food writers, and tech-savvy creatives who share a common goal: to inspire and empower individuals in their culinary journey.

Driven by our commitment to excellence, we curate a rich tapestry of recipes, cooking tips, and engaging food narratives that cater to a wide audience. Whether you're a novice in the kitchen or an experienced chef seeking fresh inspiration, EasyCooking is your go-to destination for culinary enlightenment. With a finger on the pulse of the latest culinary trends and a dedication to fostering a supportive online community, we invite you to embark on a flavorful adventure with us. Join EasyCooking, where every click is a step towards unlocking the full potential of your kitchen prowess and savoring the boundless delights of the culinary world.

Our history

Established in 2010, EasyCooking emerged as a humble endeavor born out of a shared love for accessible and delightful culinary experiences. Our journey began with a simple yet ambitious mission: to demystify the art of cooking and make it an enjoyable pursuit for everyone. Over the years, we've evolved into a thriving online haven for cooking enthusiasts, cultivating a repository of easy-to-follow recipes, time-saving cooking hacks, and expert tips. From our early days as a small blog, we have grown into a dynamic platform that empowers individuals to create delicious meals with confidence. (Here's more about the story) EasyCooking remains dedicated to its founding principles, continuously adapting to the ever-changing culinary landscape while remaining a steadfast companion on the diverse paths of home cooking exploration.

How we choose our recipes

Our recipes try to keep in mind the busy lives of everyday people, while including healthy and easy to cook meals for everyone. We believe in a healthy, balanced diet that includes everything(Buter, fruits, vegetables, protein...and all the sweets too!)

There are three things we keep in mind for all our recipes:

How easy is it to cook? We want to guarantee that everyone, regardless of culinary abilities, can cook these recipes.

Is it healthy? We want to make it easier for busy people to eat nutritious food that will benefit their health.

Finally, is it tasty? There is no point to healthy for the sake of healthy, a meal that isn't tasty is pointless to us.

To make sure each of these standards are met, we work with nutritionists, chefs and customer feedback - every angle is covered to make sure we offer the best service possible. We also have a rigorous process checking every single line of a recipe to make sure it is as efficient and easy as possible.

Additionally, at EasyCooking, we aspire to provide a human service, which means that we rigorously check submissions for AI and we guarantee that every recipe on this website has be written by a human.
