Contact Us

Do you want to get in touch with us? If you have comments on some of our recipes, or a recipe you wish to share, we would love to hear from you: contact@easycooking.com

For press inquiries, email us at contactpress@easycooking.com

If you are contacting us regarding a job posting from LinkedIn or another job posting site, note that every correspondence with a member of our Team will exclusively be through emails ending with @easycooking.com.

Write for Us

We are always eager to work with writers and new voices to join our team. Please submit pitches of four articles as examples of your published work (print or online) as well as a brief bio to writeforus@easycooking.com

  • We accept recipes coherent with the messaging of EasyCooking
  • We do not accept restaurant reviews or dining experiences stories
  • Please check that the recipes have not already been covered on our website

Please note that we do not accept unsolicited guest posts or articles
